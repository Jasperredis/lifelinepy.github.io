Lifeline.py is a retro-style endless game consisting of a
player on a line, where they can only move left and right. They
must get heals to prevent death when life is slowly ticking down.
Enemies, however, block their path.

Proudly presented by jasperredis (https://jris.straw.page)
in open source.

This website contains install/GitHub links and online information
for Lifeline.py to fetch.
